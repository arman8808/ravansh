Ravansh International

Ravansh International, headquartered in Dubai, stands as a leading trading company specializing in Gold and Cut & Polish Diamonds. Upholding core values of accountability, integrity, and reliability, the company, under the directorship of Mr. Umar Bin Basir, has earned unparalleled acclaim in the Gold and diamond trading arena in Dubai. Renowned as one of the top contenders in the industry, Ravansh International is committed to delivering genuine and flawless Gold bullion with 999 percent purity, solidifying its position as the premier destination in Dubai for acquiring these timeless metals.

Over the years, the company has navigated the dynamic landscape of the global precious metals and stones market, adapting to advancements and competitive trends. Ravansh International remains dedicated to providing the highest quality and distinctive services to its customers, consistently progressing towards significant achievements in the precious metals and stones sector.

Our Mission

Ravansh International's mission is to be a trusted and efficient precious metal and Gold trading company, prioritizing customer needs while upholding the highest levels of integrity and honesty in all business transactions. The company is committed to full compliance with applicable regulatory requirements, aiming to increase sales volume through a deep understanding of market fluctuations and fostering client relationships through proactive market monitoring and teamwork.

Our Vision

The company envisions setting up a new gold refinery and becoming a leading business entity in bullion trading in Dubai. Embracing online business bullion trading with a focus on spot transactions, Ravansh International aims to leverage the most appropriate technical capabilities and skills to emerge as a leader in the Gold and precious metals trading sector. The company aspires to deal with customers who share similar values of integrity and ethics and maintains a commitment to a high level of honesty in all internal and external business transactions.

LOGISTICS

Ravansh International offers a seamless logistics experience through its platform, global reach of partner offices, and strategic alliances. The company's logistics partnerships include comprehensive insurance coverage for all shipments, underwritten by the prestigious Lloyds Group of London, ensuring the utmost security and reliability in the transportation of precious metals.

Our aim is to rapidly grow and expand its business in UAE & in International markets.
